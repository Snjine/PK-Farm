Research Report

This report outlines the market research findings for PK Farm. The research aimed at understanding consumer preferences, competitor strategies, and market trends. The findings suggest that there is a growing demand for organic produce, especially among health-conscious urban consumers, with a gap in regional markets for premium organic products.

Financial Report

The financial aspect of the market research reveals that the budget for conducting the research is approximately 2 million KES. A significant portion of this budget is allocated towards data collection, with secondary costs for data analysis, reporting, and presentation. The financial outlook indicates that the investment in the research will lead to better market penetration and increased sales in the coming years.

Operational Report

The operational plan focuses on executing the market research over a 5-week timeline. The key phases include planning, data collection, analysis, reporting, and presentation. A dedicated team has been assigned to ensure smooth execution, with clear roles and responsibilities outlined for each phase. Risk management strategies have also been put in place to mitigate delays and ensure high-quality results.

Marketing Report

The marketing strategy derived from the market research aims to increase brand awareness and expand PK Farm's presence in both urban and regional markets. Digital marketing efforts, such as social media campaigns, influencer collaborations, and content marketing, are key to attracting health-conscious consumers. The report also outlines retail marketing strategies, community engagement initiatives, and regional expansion into neighboring countries.
