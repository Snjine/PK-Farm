EcoGrow Dynamics - Market Research Plan

1. Research Objectives

  • Identify high-demand and profitable crops
  • Understand consumer and buyer preferences
  • Analyze market competition
  • Explore gaps and opportunities for growth

2. Scope of Research

  • Geographic focus: Local to regional markets
  • Product focus: Vegetables, fruits, herbs, staples
  • Stakeholders: Consumers, retailers, wholesalers, chefs

3. Key Questions

  • What are the most demanded crops and prices?
  • What quality/variety is preferred?
  • What are the seasonal trends?
  • Who are the competitors and their strategies?

4. Methodology

Primary:
  • Surveys and interviews
  • Market observations
  • Focus groups
Secondary:
  • Government reports
  • Agri boards and NGO publications
  • Competitor websites and social media

5. Tools

  • Google Forms / KoboToolbox
  • Interview guides
  • Observation checklist
  • Spreadsheets for price tracking

7. Stakeholder Engagement

  • Offer small incentives (samples, discounts, reports)
  • Keep surveys brief and respectful
  • Build rapport and be transparent

8. Outputs

  • Market Trends Report (PDF)
  • Competitor Analysis Table
  • Customer & Demand Matrix
  • Product & Pricing Recommendations

9. Future Application

  • Guide planting and production planning
  • Refine product packaging and sales approach
  • Support partnerships and marketing campaigns
