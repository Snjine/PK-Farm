Research Plan for PK Farm

Objective: Conduct market research to gather data on consumer preferences, competitor landscape, and opportunities for PK Farm to improve its product offerings and expand market share in Kenya.

Research Methodology: The research will utilize surveys, interviews, focus groups, and field visits.

Target Audience: Consumers (urban & rural), agricultural wholesalers, retail grocery stores, and competitors.

Data Collection Methods: Online surveys, in-person interviews, focus groups, and observations at farmers' markets and trade expos.

Timeline: 5 weeks for completion, with specific phases outlined for planning, data collection, analysis, and presentation.

Financial Plan for Market Research

Estimated Costs:

  • Survey Tools: KES 20,000 - 30,000
  • Focus Group Incentives: KES 10,000 - 15,000 per group
  • Data Analysis Tools (SPSS/Excel): KES 10,000 - 20,000
  • Transport & Field Expenses: KES 15,000 - 20,000
  • Research Team Salaries: KES 50,000 - 70,000
  • Miscellaneous Costs: KES 5,000 - 10,000

Total Estimated Budget: KES 170,000 - 245,000

Operational Plan for PK Farm Market Research

Research Team: Project Manager, Data Collection Team, Data Analyst, Communications Specialist

Timeline: The research will span 5 weeks, with tasks broken down into 5 phases: Planning & Design, Data Collection, Data Analysis, Reporting, and Presentation.

Key Actions: The plan includes finalizing tools, distributing surveys, conducting interviews, and analyzing data to create actionable insights.

Resources Required: Survey tools, focus group incentives, transport costs, data analysis software, and research team salaries.

Risk Management: Strategies to address delays in data collection, low response rates, and inadequate data analysis tools.

Marketing Plan for PK Farm’s Market Research

Objective: To use the findings from the market research to raise awareness, attract new customers, and strengthen PK Farm’s position in the market.

Target Audience: Internal stakeholders (management), external stakeholders (customers, suppliers), media, and industry partners.

Key Messages: PK Farm is committed to consumer-driven growth, sustainability, and innovation, using research to improve products and meet customer demands.

Marketing Strategies: Press releases, digital marketing (social media, email), focus group discussions, trade show participation, and paid advertising campaigns.

Budget: Estimated costs for marketing activities range from KES 170,000 to KES 245,000.
