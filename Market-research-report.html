EcoGrow Dynamics

Market Research Report – Agricultural Products

April 2025

1. Executive Summary

  • High demand for cabbages and tomatoes in urban markets.
  • Seasonal price spikes offer revenue opportunities.
  • Wholesalers prefer consistent, bulk deliveries.

2. Objectives

  • Identify high-demand crops.
  • Track price trends over the year.
  • Understand competition and buyer expectations.
  • Provide recommendations for market entry and profit growth.

3. Methodology

  • 15 vendor interviews, 10 wholesalers, 5 retailers.
  • Surveyed Nairobi, Kisumu, Eldoret.
  • Used online and in-person market visits.

4. Market Overview

  • Main products: Cabbage, Tomato, Onion, Nightshade, Amaranth.
  • Organic produce gaining traction.
  • Rising interest in pre-packaged and branded produce.

5. Price Trends (Jan–Mar 2025)

Product Min (KES/kg) Max (KES/kg) Avg (KES/kg)
Cabbage 25 45 35
Tomato 40 80 60
Onion 30 55 42
Nightshade 50 85 67

6. Competitor Analysis

Competitor Strengths Weaknesses
GreenFarms Ltd Consistent volume supply Higher price point
Local Vendors Fresh, cheap produce Irregular supply
Organic Farmers Health-conscious branding Small-scale distribution

7. Stakeholder Feedback

  • Retailers want graded, washed produce.
  • Wholesalers prioritize shelf life and transport packaging.
  • Consumers are asking for chemical-free crops.

8. SWOT Analysis

Strengths Weaknesses
Fertile soils, strategic location No cold storage
Opportunities Threats
Urban demand for organic vegetables Unpredictable weather patterns

9. Recommendations

  • Focus on cabbage and tomato supply chains.
  • Improve post-harvest handling with grading and packaging.
  • Explore bulk supply to retailers and restaurants.
  • Consider branding and organic certification.
